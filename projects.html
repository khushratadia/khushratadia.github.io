3D Printed Clock Mechanism

Objectives

Design and manufacture a fully functional mechanical wall clock using modern CAD tools and 3D printing technology. Integrate traditional horological principles with contemporary manufacturing methods to create a precision timepiece.

Outcomes & Contributions

  • Successfully designed and 3D printed a functional clock mechanism
  • Implemented Geneva mechanism for precise time regulation
  • Achieved smooth mechanical operation with minimal friction
  • Demonstrated expertise in design for additive manufacturing (DfAM)
  • Created comprehensive motion studies and assembly documentation

Technical Details & Skills

Tools: SolidWorks CAD, Motion Analysis, 3D Printing (FDM)

Key Components:

  • Geneva mechanism with 4:1 gear ratio for hour hand
  • Deadbeat escapement for precise second intervals
  • Rack-and-pinion weight drive system
  • Custom gear trains with calculated tooth profiles
  • Magnetic pendulum stabilization system

Manufacturing: All components designed for single-print assembly with 0.2mm tolerance requirements
